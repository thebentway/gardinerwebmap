What’s Missing?

What is also important to consider is the stories that are not told by this map. The space under the Gardiner Expressway has historically been, and continues to be, a place for respite and refuge for many people, including for unhoused populations.

We note that as we plan for the future of the space under the Gardiner, we must aim to meet the needs of all Torontonians and learn from this critical community.
